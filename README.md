## Purpose

This account exists as a working notebook for curiosity. It is not a portfolio of finished products, but a record of an ongoing process: testing ideas, building small experiments, and learning by doing.

```
> mission
  - experience new concepts and record the knowledge along the way
  - explore creative and unconventional solutions
  - follow curiosity into new domains, methods, and tools
  - treat learning as active and iterative, not passive
```

## Approach

Work here is exploratory by nature. Repositories may represent early-stage experiments, partial investigations, or attempts that did not fully succeed — all treated as valid parts of the research process, not exceptions to it.
